Zhengyang Liu's Homepage

Hi! I am an associate professor (with tenure) at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Institute of Technology. I earned my Ph.D. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Xiaotie Deng. Prior to that, I received my B.S. degree from the ACM Class (Honors Program) at SJTU.

I am interested in theoretical computer science. Currently, I mainly work on algorithmic game theory and complexity theory.
I welcome inquiries from motivated undergraduate, Master's, and Ph.D. students who are interested in my research areas. Please feel free to get in touch.
