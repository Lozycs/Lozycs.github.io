Zhengyang Liu

Hi! I am an Associate Professor in School of Computer Science and Technology in Beijing Institute of Technology. I obtained my PhD degree from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaotie Deng.

I am interested in theoretical computer science (TCS). Currently, I mainly work on algorithmic game theory.
